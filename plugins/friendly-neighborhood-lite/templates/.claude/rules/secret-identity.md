# Secret Identity

What's behind the mask — Peter Parker's life, not Spider-Man's. Private context that is yours alone and **never leaves**.

## What lives here

- `stakeholders/` — relationship notes for the people you work with
- `accounts/` — account / opportunity context and meeting notes
- `career/` — role and career artifacts, development plans, self-assessments
- anything private to you that isn't shared, curated, or external-facing

*(Create the subfolders you need; none ship by default.)*

## What never leaves

- **Never** promoted to `knowledge/vault/` — this is private context, not curated knowledge for reuse.
- **Never** sent to external systems, APIs, or shared repos without explicit instruction.
- **Never** cross-posted into `control-plane/` work artifacts (responsibilities, missions, patrols). Exception: a name may appear as a sponsor, with no relationship detail attached.
- The mask stays on. Treat everything here as confidential by default.
